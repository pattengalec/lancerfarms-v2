About the Farm

Lancer Farms & Gardens is a hands-on learning space where CBU students, faculty, and community members grow vegetables, herbs, and fruit together. The farm is maintained by student caretakers and is open to all who wish to learn, contribute, or simply enjoy the beauty of growing things.
