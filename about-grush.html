a management platform for growing spaces

grush turns the daily work of running a growing space — a garden, a farm, a controlled-environment grow — into something simple enough to use with dirt on your hands.

Tasks, plantings, harvests, inventory, and records become a tap-not-type system anyone on the ground can run. Over time, that daily work turns into real data: what was planted, when, where, and how it did.

Deployments

Every place that runs grush is a deployment — branded as its own, running on the same engine underneath.

Lancer Farms is a live deployment of grush — a working teaching garden in Riverside, California.
